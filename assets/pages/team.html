Team

United by pasion
Perfected by skill

Our team is a family of culinary artists and visionaries, led by the renowned Chef Hiroshi Tanaka. Each member brings a unique depth of knowledge, technique, and passion that embodies the spirit of Kōyō. With a shared commitment to precision and artistry, our team is dedicated to creating an unforgettable dining experience that celebrates the harmony and elegance of Japanese cuisine.

We invite you to discover the seamless collaboration that defines every dish, every moment, and every memory at Kōyō.
